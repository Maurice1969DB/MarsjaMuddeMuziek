### Utrecht Summer School: Hildegard von Bingen

Marsja will be a guest teacher at the [Utrecht University Summer School on the music of Hildegard von Bingen](https://utrechtsummerschool.nl/courses/art-music/hildegard-von-bingen-medieval-chant) in July 2026. This international, interdisciplinary yet introductory course runs from 13–17 July and offers an intensive week of academic lectures and chant workshops exploring Hildegard's repertoire. The week concludes with an informal performance in a medieval church in Utrecht. Registration open until 31 May 2026.
