    Wat deelnemers zeggen

    Hildegard von Bingen workshops & lessen

    “I can’t begin to describe what a joy it is to study with Marsja Mudde in private lessons and in her Hildegard Study Days! She doesn’t just bring technical expertise to the classroom; her whole being exudes this music! I feel not only like I am becoming a better musician, but also a better person when studying with her. Highly recommend.”

    — Angela Rocchio

    “Marsja was able to demonstrate how to integrate von Bingen’s music in a full bodied way, using the text, the human heartbeat, and shapes to give beautiful lines to the music. I’ve never been to a talk that was equal parts mystical and practical, which was a lovely surprise. I’d love to hear more from Marsja.”

    — Eileen C. ~ professional singer and cathedral cantor, Massachusetts

    “The class was not just about reading music, it was about bringing Hildegard’s process of composing to life. Her knowledge of Hildegard’s music was much deeper than anything I had experienced before, going beyond notation and into the realm of emotion and inspiration.”

    — Miyoko ~ Germany

    “Instead of just laying down a ’this is how it is,’ Marsja made me feel empowered to THINK, and that makes me much more excited about learning more. I liked the way we got a taste of the depth of her expertise, with the way she could come up with additional examples to illustrate any point, while at the same time she was sincerely willing to teach the basics. She gave a genuinely ‘academic’ (knowledge-seeking, collegial) feeling to the course, instead of a didactic or performance-oriented one.”

    — Hanne G. ~ parish music director, New York

    “I’m doing a Hildegard sing this fall, by popular demand, and I would not have attempted it without the information in this course. Marsja is skilled at connecting vocal technique to the performance and meaning of Hildegard’s music. I have more freedom as a soloist and a better connection to our ensemble because of her teaching.”

    — Erika Singer ~ founder of The Laetare Project, Washington D.C.
