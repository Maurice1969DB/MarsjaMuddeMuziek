Hildegard Study Days Online via International Chant Academy

In collaboration with the International Chant Academy in the USA, Marsja teaches online interactive sessions on the music of Hildegard von Bingen.

About these sessions

Each live, unique, online 2-hour session is devoted to unpacking 1-2 of Hildegard’s musical compositions. The sessions are interactive and conducted via Zoom, with a small class size to maximize teacher/student interaction.

What you’ll learn

  • The unique musical language of Hildegard von Bingen
  • Interpretation of medieval notation
  • Performance practice for this extraordinary music
  • The rich texts and layers of meaning in Hildegard’s compositions

Practical information

  • Format: Live online via Zoom
  • Duration: 2 hours per session
  • Language: English
  • Suitable for: Singers of all levels with an interest in medieval music
