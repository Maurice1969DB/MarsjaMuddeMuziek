PROGRAMS


    Marsja Mudde Muziek is engaged in solo and ensemble concerts. At the moment Marsja can be heard and seen in the following programs:

  • Civitas
    Civitas Civitas is a project of Marsja Mudde Muziek. Civitas means city and refers to the heavenly city of Jerusalem, the city of peace with a dazzling beauty, but also to the earthly Jerusalem and the many other pilgrimage cities in Europe.

  • Vita Hildegardis Bingenis
    Vita Hildegardis Bingensis Vita Hildegardis Bingensis is a concert series with the new ensemble Anime Beatitudinis Cantando with the singers Agnieszka Chabowska, Cora Schmeiser and Marsja Mudde.
